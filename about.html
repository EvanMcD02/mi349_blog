About Me

My name is Evan McDonnell. I am a Game Developer at Michigan State University. I am majoring in Games and Interactive Media with a minor in Information Technology. I also participate in several student organizations on campus such as:

  • Spartan Brass
  • Spartan Marching Band
  • Spartan Dischords Acapella

I am a senior in the university and my expected graduation is Spring of 2025! I want to go into game design and development after I graduate, and eventually I want to create music for video games.

Music has always been a large passion of mine and it shows in all of my extra groups that I'm a part of on campus. I love arranging music for marching bands, and digital music as well. I play saxophones, trumpet, piano, and ukelele. I also have a strong passion for music theory, and one day I would love to be a well known music composer.

Thank you for visiting my page! I look forward to sharing more of my journey with you.
